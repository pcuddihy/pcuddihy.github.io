Overview: My project is examining how COVID-19 has affected San Francisco. I am using data to show how different groups across San Francisco have been affected.
For my project, I am going to start with the most general level and get more specific as I go, showing what communities have been affected the most.

Completed Features: So far, I have completed all of the bar charts in my project and have implemented them with tooltips that show more detail.

Upcoming Milestones: I plan on working on the line charts, which will include multiple tracking lines showing the amount of cases for each age group, race, and the whole city.

Roadblocks: Nothing right now, though I am thinking about how to change my overall design and color choice to liven up the charts.
